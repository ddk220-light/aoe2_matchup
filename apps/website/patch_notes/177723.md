Balance highlights from Update 177723 that affect unit combat in our simulator. See the **Official notes** link above for the full changelog.

## Units (all civs)

- **Slinger** cost 70 food / 10 wood -> **50 food / 25 wood**; **+2 conversion resistance**.

## Aztecs

- **Xolotl Warrior** now benefits from **6 additional technologies**.

## Armenians

- **Elite Composite Bowman** HP 45 -> **50**.
- Civilization **gains the Siege Ram**.

## Incas

- Food discount per age 5 / 10 / 15 / 20% -> **15 / 20 / 25 / 30%**.
- **Kamayuk** food cost adjusted so the net cost is **unchanged** after the larger discount.

## Muisca

- Settlement Wonder discount 25% -> **33%**.
- **(Elite) Temple Guard** cost 80 food / 45 gold -> **70 food / 45 gold**; anti-cavalry bonus +3 -> **+5** (Elite +6 -> **+8**).
- **(Elite) Guecha Warrior** HP 50 -> **55** (Elite 50 -> **60**).
- **Huaracas** (Slinger train-time bonus) 25% -> **50%**.

## Tupi

- **Blackwood Archer** train time 14s -> **18s** (Elite unchanged).

## Wei

- **(Elite) Tiger Cavalry** HP 115 -> **110** (Elite 130 -> **125**); train time 15s -> **18s**.

## Champi Warrior line

- Train time 45 / 40 / 26 / 21 -> **42 / 34 / 26 / 21**.
- Runner upgrade 120 food / 60 gold -> **90 food / 60 gold**; research 50s -> **40s**.
- Warrior upgrade 200 food / 175 gold -> **150 food / 175 gold**.

## Economy & naval (no unit-vs-unit matchup impact)

- **Burgundians** economic upgrade discount 33% -> 40%; relic food 30 -> 20 / min.
- **Mapuche** foragers work 20% -> 25% faster.
- **Docks / warships** garrison arrows 5 -> 0 / 3 / 4 / 5 by age; dock bonus vs ships +3 -> +0 / 2 / 3 / 4 by age. Tower anti-ship attack reduced.
