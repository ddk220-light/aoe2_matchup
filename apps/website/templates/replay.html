AoE2 Replay Analyzer

Watch any Age of Empires II match play back on an isometric map — every unit auto-classified and player-coloured, with the full build order, tech timeline, and army movements reconstructed from the recorded game. Upload an .aoe2record file or pull a match straight from a player's recent games.

  • Isometric playback with classified unit sprites, terrain, resources & buildings
  • Full build-order and technology timeline
  • Military, economy, APM and production trackers
  • Play/pause, step, 1×–16× speed, scrub, zoom & pan
  • Search players & load matches from the AoE2 Companion API
  • One-click WebM highlight clip of the biggest engagement
  • Deep-link sharing to an exact match and timestamp

How to use it

  1. Click Upload and choose an .aoe2record file, or search a player and pick a recent match.
  2. Press play (or Space) to watch the game; scrub the timeline or change speed to focus on key fights.
  3. Hit Export clip to generate a shareable WebM of the biggest engagement.
