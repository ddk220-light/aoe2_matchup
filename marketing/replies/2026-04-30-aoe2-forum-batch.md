### Paste this:

> You're Britons — you actually have one of the best toolkits in the game against Tupi. Three things to know:
>
> **Longbows hard-counter Blackwood Archers.** Range 12 vs 8 means you outshoot them at distance. In an equal-cost mass fight (4500 resources each side, fully upgraded) Longbows finish at 60-0 with ~100% HP remaining. The mistake most Britons players make is engaging at the BWA's range — stay at max Longbow range and they can't even fire back.
>
> **Imperial Skirms are even more cost-efficient against BWAs.** They're so cheap you field nearly 3× more, and BWA pierce armor is near zero. Equal-cost: ~74-0 with 99% HP for Imp Skirm. If you're gold-starved, mass Imp Skirms.
>
> **Don't make Halberdiers against Tupi.** Halbs only have anti-cav bonus damage — vs the Tupi Blackwood Archer they lose 0-56 at equal cost (BWA 92% HP). Vs the Ibirapema Warrior they lose 0-50 (Ibira 72% HP — trample shreds Halbs). Halbs do nothing here.
>
> The full Britons answer is **Longbow + Imp Skirm + a few Champions for closers** (Champions beat BWAs at equal cost). Skip the cavalry stable entirely — Britons cav isn't great anyway.
>
> If you want to test other matchups: I built a battle simulator at https://aoe2matchup.com — every unit fully upgraded, run any 1v1 or army fight against any other unit.
