## Ability

_Charged ranged attack every 30 seconds except when in melee combat_

When fighting units (except siege weapons), Fire Lancers can shoot a volley of three projectiles with low accuracy in a range of 4 (thus being better vs groups of units). Each projectile deals 3 pierce attack (unaffected by armor) and +2 vs . The ability recharges in 30 seconds.

Despite not being able to target siege weapons and buildings, the projectiles may hit them. Its armor-ignoring ability does not work against siege units, ships, and buildings, just like other armor-ignoring abilities.
