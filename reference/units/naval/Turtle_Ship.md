## Ability

_Can attack ground_

thumb|left|Cannon and rocket attacks demonstrated against a [[Thirisadai]]
Since update 141935, it has two attack types, firing cannonballs and volleys of rockets automatically. It first shoots its cannon (does not fire rockets simultaneously with the cannonball), then releases two volleys of rockets; this constitutes one attack cycle. A charge meter shows up above the Turtle Ship indicating the time left until it shoots another cannonball.

When using Attack Ground, they fire both rockets and cannonballs.
